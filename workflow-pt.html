Phase 0 — Before We Start
1
You + Us

Agreement Signed & Account Created

We send you a simple service agreement outlining the scope, pricing, and term. Once signed, you'll pay the setup fee and we get started. We also walk you through creating a dedicated outreach email on your domain (e.g., referrals@yourpractice.com) — this is the address all outreach goes out from. Takes about 5 minutes to set up.

We handle all technical configuration — DKIM, SPF, and domain authentication. No technical knowledge required. We send your IT team copy-paste instructions; they're done in 20 minutes.

Domain warmup: New sending addresses need about 2 weeks of warmup before emailing at full volume — this is standard practice and keeps your deliverability high. We start warmup the moment your account is created, running quietly in the background while we build your list and write your sequences. By the time sequences are approved, the domain is ready to go.

⏱ ~15 min total, one time
2
You

Set Up Your Booking Link

Create a free Calendly account, connect it to your calendar, and share your booking link with us. When an interested physician wants to meet, they book directly onto your calendar — ArgusReach sends the link, Calendly handles the rest. You get the confirmation, they get the confirmation. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Calendly works with Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Office 365, iCloud, and Exchange. Whatever your practice uses, it's supported.

⏱ ~5–10 min, one time
Phase 1 — Setup · Week 1
3
You

Intake Form — 10 minutes

We learn your practice through a short intake form: your specialties, target physicians, geography, tone, and how you communicate. Takes about 10 minutes. Everything after this is handled for you.

⏱ ~10 min, one time
4
You

Share Two Lists — Optional but Valuable

Anyone who should never receive a cold email — existing referral partners, current relationships, competitors — goes on your do-not-contact list. We remove them automatically before anything sends.

Can be a spreadsheet, a CSV from your EHR, or even a rough list of names. We clean and organize it.

⏱ ~15 min if your contacts are organized; longer if you're pulling from multiple sources. No rush — send it over whenever it's ready.
5
Argus

Build Your Physician Target List

Argus sources verified physician contacts in your target area — PCPs, orthopedic surgeons, sports medicine doctors, neurologists, or whatever specialties match your practice. Every contact is verified: name, practice, email, specialty. Your do-not-contact list is cross-referenced automatically — existing referral partners and anyone off-limits never receive an email.

⏱ 1–2 business days
6
Argus

Write Personalized Outreach Sequences

A 4–5 touch email sequence in your voice — introducing your practice, your specialties, and why a referral relationship makes sense. Each email's opening line is written specifically for that physician — referencing their specialty, practice type, or location. Not a template blast. Every message feels like it was written for them.

What's a sequence? A sequence is a series of short, timed follow-up emails sent automatically over 3–4 weeks. Most people don't respond to the first email — not because they're not interested, but because they're busy. A sequence follows up at natural intervals so no opportunity falls through the cracks. Each email is a standalone message, not a reply chain — every one is short, professional, and easy to respond to.

⏱ 1–2 business days
7
You + Us

Review and Approve

We share the complete email sequence with you before a single email sends. Read through it, edit anything, change the tone — whatever you need. Nothing goes out without your explicit sign-off. Most clients take 15–20 minutes on this step.

⏱ ~15–20 min
Phase 2 — Live · Week 2 onward
8
Argus

Campaigns Go Live

Emails send from your outreach address on a human-like schedule — not a blast, not a bot pattern. Follow-ups go out automatically to non-responders at timed intervals. Every email looks like it came directly from you.

⏱ Runs automatically, 24/7
9
Argus

Every Reply Is Handled

Argus monitors and classifies every reply in real time:

Interested → booking link sent immediately. The physician books directly onto your calendar and you receive an instant Calendly confirmation — name, practice, and meeting time. No action needed from you.
Not now → logged with a follow-up date, re-queued for a future sequence.
Unsubscribe → honored instantly, contact added to permanent do-not-contact.
Anything unusual → flagged to you directly, no auto-response.

⏱ Typically within the hour
10
Argus

Every Touchpoint Tracked Automatically

Every contact is logged: when we reached out, what was sent, whether they opened it, what they replied, and what the next action is. No more guessing when you last contacted someone or whether a physician has ever been in your pipeline. Your entire referral outreach history — organized, searchable, always current.

⏱ Updated in real time
11
You

Show Up to the Meeting

The physician already knows who you are — they responded to your email. You walk in warm, not cold. The meeting is on your calendar with context on who they are, what they replied, and any prior outreach history.

⏱ Your only recurring commitment
Phase 3 — Ongoing · Every Month
12
Argus

Monthly Report + Optimization

Every month you receive a clear report covering: emails sent, open rate, positive replies, meetings booked, contacts pending follow-up, and what we're changing next month based on what worked. No jargon — just the numbers that matter and a plain-English summary of what's happening in your pipeline. We optimize continuously: subject lines, timing, messaging angles. The system gets sharper every cycle.

⏱ ~5 min to read
13
Argus

Pipeline Refreshes Automatically

As the contact list works through the sequence, Argus sources new physician contacts to keep volume consistent. Your pipeline never goes quiet. You never have to think about it.

⏱ Automatic, every month
