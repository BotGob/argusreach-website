The Problem

You shouldn't be spending your time prospecting.

Most professional services firms built their client base on referrals. Referrals are great — until they plateau. They're unpredictable, unscalable, and entirely outside your control.

The alternative — consistent outbound — takes time you don't have. You shouldn't be spending your days researching prospects and writing cold emails. That's not what you're great at. Conversations and relationships are.

Argus handles everything before the meeting. You handle everything after. That's the division of labor that wins.

How It Works

One intake form. Then just show up.

01

We learn your business

A 10-minute intake form to define your ideal client, your voice, your goals. After that, you're mostly done.

02

We build your prospect list

We source verified contacts matching your exact criteria — titles, company size, geography, trigger events. Your do-not-contact list is cross-referenced automatically — existing clients, partners, and anyone off-limits are excluded before anything sends.

03

We write and send the outreach — as you

Every email is personalized to the individual. It goes out from your name, your email address, your firm. Prospects never know we're involved. You review and approve the sequences before a single email sends.

04

We handle every reply

Interested prospects get routed to your calendar automatically. Not-now replies get logged with a follow-up date. Unsubscribe requests are honored immediately. You get notified of hot replies as they happen, and a full monthly performance report.

05

You show up to qualified meetings

The meetings on your calendar are already warm — they replied to an email from you, they know what you do, they agreed to talk. You're walking into a conversation, not a cold pitch.

Your Control

Fully managed. Runs automatically. You stay in charge.

Nothing goes out without your sign-off

We write your sequences, you review and approve them before launch. Edit anything — copy, tone, industry language. We implement changes within 24 hours. Not a single email sends until you've approved it.

Runs automatically after that

Once you approve the sequence, the system runs. Outreach sends, replies get handled, interested prospects land on your calendar. You only hear from us when someone's interested or something needs your attention.

Our team reviews all outbound activity

Every reply is reviewed and handled by our team before anything goes back out under your name. You never have to worry about a bad response going to a prospect.

Monthly reporting, full transparency

Contacts reached, replies received, meetings booked, what worked, what we're changing. You see the full picture every month.
