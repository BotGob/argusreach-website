What we do
1

We define exactly who you're targeting

A short intake form. We learn your ideal client profile - the titles, industries, life stages, and financial situations that match your best relationships. Business owners, corporate executives, pre-retirees, HNW households - whoever fits your practice, we build the targeting blueprint around them.

2

We source and verify the prospect list

We identify and verify the right contacts in your target market - people who match your ICP, in your geography, reachable by email. Your do-not-contact list is cross-referenced automatically - existing clients, centers of influence, and anyone off-limits are excluded before a single email sends.

3

We write and send outreach in your voice

A professional introduction sequence - written specifically for your practice, your market, and the type of prospect you're targeting. Every email is personalized to the individual recipient. You review and approve every sequence before launch. Outreach goes from your email address, signed as you. Prospects never know we're involved.

4

We handle every reply and book the meeting

Every response is reviewed and handled. Interested prospects receive a reply with your booking link - they pick a time from your live calendar and the meeting lands automatically. Every draft response is reviewed before it sends - nothing goes out under your name without a human eye on it. You walk into a warm conversation, not a cold pitch.

Built with compliance in mind

We know this matters for RIAs and FINRA-registered advisors. Our role is strictly prospect identification and meeting scheduling - no investment recommendations, no performance claims, no solicitation of retail investors in a regulated sense. Every email is a professional introduction, equivalent to a cold call or a conference handshake. You review and approve all sequences before launch. Your compliance team can review the copy at any point. We don't send until you've signed off.
