What we do
1

We map and size the opportunity — using public records

Government entities publish their investment balances publicly via CAFR (Comprehensive Annual Financial Reports) and bond issuance data via EMMA (the Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board's disclosure system). We use these to identify every entity in your target states within your assets under management (AUM) range — cities, counties, fire districts, police departments, school boards, utility authorities — and filter down to the right-size prospects your advisors actually want to meet with.

2

We find the right contacts and build the prospect list

Once we've sized the opportunity, we identify the actual decision-makers — Finance Directors, City Treasurers, CFOs, and comptrollers — and build a clean, verified contact list. If you already have entity relationships internally but are missing contacts, we fill the gaps. No cold database dumps — a curated, validated list built around your ideal client profile.

3

Compliance-approved outreach, sent in your advisor's voice

We write a professional email sequence that positions your firm's expertise in public entity cash management, LGIP pools, and fixed income — then your compliance team reviews and approves every word before a single email sends. Outreach goes from your advisor's email address, signed as them. We operate entirely in the background.

4

Interested prospects book directly onto your advisor's calendar

Every response gets classified and routed. When someone's interested, a Calendly link goes back immediately and the meeting lands directly on your advisor's calendar. Your advisor shows up to a qualified conversation — already knowing the entity type, estimated assets under management, and contact context. They close. We handled everything before that call.

Built with compliance in mind

We know SEC and FINRA rules matter here. Our role is strictly lead generation and meeting scheduling — no investment recommendations, no performance claims, no solicitation of retail investors. All outreach copy is submitted to your compliance team for approval before launch. We don't send a single email until you've signed off. Your firm stays in control; we just fill the calendar.
