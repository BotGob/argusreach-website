Setting Up Your App Password

Required to connect your outreach email to ArgusReach

Why do I need this? An app password lets ArgusReach send emails on your behalf from your dedicated outreach account. It is a separate password from your regular login - it only grants access to your outreach email inbox, not your Google account, Drive, calendar, or any other data. This takes about 5 minutes to set up.
📧 Gmail / Google Workspace 🔷 Microsoft 365 / Outlook
📧

Gmail / Google Workspace

For @gmail.com and Google Workspace business accounts (e.g. you@yourfirm.com)

Before you start - you will need:
  • Your dedicated outreach email address (the new one you set up, not your personal email)
  • 2-Step Verification enabled on that account (covered in Step 1 below)

Step 1 - Enable 2-Step Verification (required)

  • 1

    Go to your Google Account security settings

    Open a browser and go to the link below. Sign in with your outreach email if prompted.

    myaccount.google.com/security
  • 2

    Find "2-Step Verification" and click it

    Scroll down to the "How you sign in to Google" section. Click 2-Step Verification.

    Note: If you already see "2-Step Verification - On" you can skip directly to Step 2 below.
  • 3

    Click "Get started" and follow the prompts

    Google will walk you through setting up a second verification method. The easiest option is Google prompt (a tap on your phone) or an authenticator app. Complete the setup and confirm it is turned on.

Step 2 - Create the App Password

  • 1

    Go directly to the App Passwords page

    Click the link below. You may be asked to sign in again - use your outreach email.

    myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
    Can't find it? On the security page, use your browser's Find function (Ctrl+F or Cmd+F) and search for "App passwords".
  • 2

    Name the app password

    You will see a text field that says "App name". Type ArgusReach (or any name you will recognize) and click Create.

  • 3

    Copy your 16-character password

    Google will display a 16-character password in a yellow box. Copy it exactly as shown - spaces are not required. This is the password you will submit to ArgusReach.

    Important: This password is only shown once. Copy it now and paste it into the ArgusReach credential form before closing this window.
Google Workspace (business accounts) - extra note If your Google Workspace is managed by your company's IT administrator, app passwords may be disabled by your organization. In that case, ask your IT admin to enable app passwords for your account in the Google Admin console. If you are unsure, contact us at vito@argusreach.com and we will help you sort it out.

You are all set for Gmail

Submit your outreach email address and the 16-character app password through your ArgusReach credential link. Do not share this password anywhere else.

Microsoft 365 / Outlook

For Microsoft 365 business accounts (e.g. you@yourfirm.com)

Microsoft 365 Business - check with your IT admin first If your M365 account is managed by your company, your admin may need to enable app passwords before you can create one. If the steps below do not show an "App passwords" section, contact your IT department and ask them to enable app passwords for your account in the Microsoft 365 Admin Center under Security - Multi-Factor Authentication settings.
Before you start - you will need:
  • Your dedicated outreach email address
  • Two-step verification enabled (covered in Step 1 below)

Step 1 - Enable Two-Step Verification (required)

  • 1

    Go to your Microsoft account security settings

    Open a browser and go to the link below. Sign in with your outreach email.

    account.microsoft.com/security
  • 2

    Click "Two-step verification"

    Under the "Security" section, find Two-step verification and click Turn on. Follow the on-screen steps to set it up using the Microsoft Authenticator app or your phone number.

    Already set up? If two-step verification shows as "On", skip to Step 2 below.

Step 2 - Create the App Password

  • 1

    Go to your Microsoft 365 Security Info page

    Click the link below and sign in with your outreach email when prompted.

    mysignins.microsoft.com/security-info
    Once on the Security Info page, click + Add sign-in method, select App password from the dropdown, and follow the prompts.
  • 2

    Create the app password

    Name your app password (e.g. ArgusReach), then click Create. A password will be generated for you.

  • 3

    Copy your app password

    Microsoft will display a generated password. Copy it immediately - you will not be able to see it again after closing the window.

    Important: This password is only shown once. Copy it now and paste it into the ArgusReach credential form before closing this window.
Cannot find the App Passwords section? This usually means one of the following: (1) two-step verification is not fully enabled, (2) your account is managed by a business IT admin who has disabled app passwords, or (3) your account uses a security key as the only 2FA method. Contact your IT admin or reach out to ArgusReach support for help.

You are all set for Microsoft 365

Submit your outreach email address and the app password through your ArgusReach credential link. Do not share this password anywhere else.
