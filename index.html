How it works

Five steps.
You close deals. We fill the calendar.

At onboarding you tell us your ICP and approve the sequences. Optionally share your existing contacts and we'll warm those up first. After that, your only job is showing up to the meetings we book.

01

Define Your Ideal Customer

One onboarding call. We learn your business, your best clients, your deal size — and build the precise targeting blueprint that drives every decision we make on your behalf.

02

We Build the List — You Share What You Already Have

We source verified contacts matched to your ideal client profile. You can also bring two lists: a warm list (conference leads, referrals, lapsed contacts — we reach out to these first with openers that reference the connection) and a relationships list (existing partners, colleagues, current clients — we remove these automatically so you never cold email someone you already know). Cold sourcing covers the rest. One CSV each. We handle everything from there.

03

Write Personalized Outreach

Argus writes every email for the individual — referencing their specific company, role, and market context. Not a template. Not a mail merge. Messages that earn real responses.

04

Run Automated Follow-Up

4 to 5 touch sequences run automatically, spaced over three weeks. Most positive replies come after touch 3 or 4. We never stop too early. No opportunity is left on the table.

05

Deliver Qualified Meetings

Argus reviews every reply, qualifies interest, and routes warm conversations directly to your calendar. You close deals. The pipeline stays full.

Trust & Safety

Your reputation
is never at risk.

We communicate under your name. That's a serious responsibility. Here's exactly how we protect you.

You approve everything before launch

Every email sequence is written and sent to you for review before a single message goes out. You can edit, rewrite, or reject anything. Nothing runs without your explicit sign-off.

Every reply, handled with care

Our team can review responses before they send — so your name, your voice, and your reputation stay protected at every step.

Argus has one job — book the meeting

Argus is strictly constrained. It will never answer investment questions, discuss coverage specifics, give clinical advice, or make promises. If anything unusual comes in, it stops and alerts you immediately.

Your data stays yours

Your prospect lists, client data, and business information are used only to run your campaign. We work with vetted platforms to deliver results — your data is never sold, never shared with other clients, and never used for anything outside your engagement. All data returned to you upon request or at end of engagement.

ArgusReach is invisible

Your prospects never know we exist. Every email comes from your name, your domain. Replies come to your account. We are the system behind the scenes — you are the relationship.

Your existing relationships are protected

At onboarding, you give us a list of people you already know in your market — referral partners, colleagues, current clients. We cross-reference every prospect list against it before a single email sends. Anyone you already have a relationship with gets removed automatically. You receive a log of who was protected and why.

Every touchpoint tracked

Every contact we've reached out to, every email sent, every reply received, and the date of last contact — all logged automatically. No more guessing when you last talked to someone or whether they've been contacted before. Your entire outreach history in one place.

Full transparency, always

You receive reports showing every email sent, open rates, reply rates, and meetings booked. Nothing runs in a black box. You see exactly what's happening in your pipeline at all times.
