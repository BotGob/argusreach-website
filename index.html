How it works

Simple process.
Serious results.

An intake form to define your ideal client. We build the outreach, you approve it. Then we run the campaign - and you show up to the meetings.

01

Define Your Ideal Customer

An intake form. We learn your business, your best clients, your deal size - and build the precise targeting blueprint that drives every decision we make on your behalf.

02

Build the List. Protect Your Relationships.

We source verified contacts matched to your ideal client profile. Share a do-not-contact list at intake - existing clients, partners, anyone off-limits - and they're automatically excluded before a single email goes out.

03

Write Personalized Outreach

Argus researches each prospect and writes every email for that specific person - their company, their role, their market context. AI-generated personalization at the opening of every message, at scale. Not a template. Not a mail merge. Messages that read like you wrote them yourself.

04

We Run the Full Sequence

Most outreach stops after one or two emails. We run a complete multi-touch sequence - personalized emails spaced over two weeks, followed by a personal phone follow-up for contacts who haven't replied. Every touchpoint references the last. When someone responds, their reply is reviewed and approved before anything sends back - so your name is never at risk.

05

Deliver Qualified Meetings

Argus reviews every reply, qualifies interest, and routes warm conversations directly to your calendar. You close deals. The pipeline stays full.

Trust & Safety

Your reputation
is never at risk.

We communicate under your name. That's a serious responsibility. Here's exactly how we protect you.

You approve the sequence before launch

Every email sequence is written and sent to you for review before a single message goes out. You can edit, rewrite, or reject anything. Nothing runs without your explicit sign-off.

Every reply, handled with care

Our team can review responses before they send - so your name, your voice, and your reputation stay protected at every step.

Argus has one job - book the meeting

Argus is strictly constrained. It will never answer investment questions, discuss coverage specifics, give clinical advice, or make promises. If anything unusual comes in, it stops and alerts you immediately.

Your data stays yours

Your prospect lists, client data, and business information are used only to run your campaign. We work with vetted platforms to deliver results - your data is never sold, never shared with other clients, and never used for anything outside your engagement. All data returned to you upon request or at end of engagement.

ArgusReach is invisible

Your prospects never know we exist. Every email comes from your name, your domain. Replies come to your account. We are the system behind the scenes - you are the relationship.

Your existing relationships are protected

At onboarding, you give us a list of people you already know in your market - referral partners, colleagues, current clients. We cross-reference every prospect list against it before a single email sends. Anyone you already have a relationship with gets removed automatically.

Every touchpoint tracked

Every contact we've reached out to, every email sent, every reply received, and the date of last contact - all logged automatically. No more guessing when you last talked to someone or whether they've been contacted before. Your entire outreach history in one place.

Full transparency, always

Every month you receive a plain-English activity summary: contacts reached, replies received, and meetings booked. Nothing runs in a black box. You see exactly what's happening in your pipeline at all times.
