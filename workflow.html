Phase 0 - Getting Started
1
You + Us

Agreement & Setup Fee

After your strategy call, we send a simple service agreement covering scope, pricing, and term. Once signed and the $500 setup fee is paid, we send you an intake form and get started.

⏱ ~5 min to sign and pay
2
You

Fill Out Your Intake Form

We learn your business through a short form - who your best clients are, who you want to reach, your target market, and how you communicate. No call required. Everything after this is handled for you.

⏱ ~10 min, one time
3
You

Share Your Do-Not-Contact List - Optional

Anyone you already work with, have an active relationship with, or never want contacted. We scrub the entire list before anything sends. Can be a spreadsheet, a CSV, or a rough list of names - we take care of it.

⏱ ~10 min if you have one - no rush
Phase 1 - Setup · Weeks 1–2
4
Argus

We Build Your Prospect List

We source verified contacts matched to the ideal client profile from your intake form - name, company, title, and email confirmed. Every address is validated before it ever enters a campaign. Your do-not-contact list is cross-referenced automatically.

⏱ 1–2 business days
5
Argus

We Write Your Outreach Sequences

A 3-touch email sequence written in your voice. Each email's opening is personalized to that specific prospect - referencing their company or context so it reads like it was written for them, not copied from a template. Short, direct, and easy to respond to.

What's a sequence? A short series of timed follow-up emails sent automatically over a few weeks. Most people don't respond to the first email - not because they're uninterested, but because they're busy. The sequence follows up naturally so no opportunity slips through.

⏱ 1–2 business days
6
You + Us

You Review and Approve

We share the full sequence before a single email sends. Read through it, adjust the tone, change anything - it's your name on every email. Nothing goes out without your approval.

⏱ ~15–20 min
7
You

Set Up Your Outreach Email

You create a dedicated sending address on your domain - something like outreach@yourfirm.com - using Google Workspace or Microsoft 365. This keeps your main inbox completely separate and is the address all outreach sends from.

We send you the exact DNS records to forward to whoever manages your domain. Most IT teams add them in under 20 minutes. Once they're in, we verify everything and confirm you're good to go.

⏱ ~10 min to create the address - IT adds DNS records separately
8
You

Connect Your Calendar

We set up a dedicated booking link for you - no extra accounts needed on your end. You connect your existing Google or Outlook calendar. Prospects see your real availability and pick a time directly. You choose how you meet: phone, video, in-person at your office, or you go to them.

⏱ ~2 min to connect
9
Argus

Warmup Period - Runs in the Background

Before any outreach sends, your new email address spends 2–3 weeks building its sending reputation automatically. This is what ensures your emails land in the inbox - not spam - from day one. You don't do anything during this window. It runs entirely in the background while everything else is being finalized.

⏱ 2–3 weeks, fully automated
Phase 2 - Live · Week 3–5 Onward
10
Argus

Campaign Goes Live

Once warmup is complete and everything is verified, outreach begins. Emails send on a natural human schedule - not a bulk blast. Follow-ups go out automatically to non-responders at timed intervals. Every email looks like it came directly from you. Monthly billing begins on this date.

⏱ Runs automatically, 24/7
11
Argus

Every Reply Is Handled

Argus monitors and classifies every reply in real time:

Interested → a reply draft is prepared and reviewed before it sends. The prospect receives a response with your booking link, picks a time from your live calendar, and books. You get a notification with their name, company, and meeting details.

Not now → logged and re-queued for a future touchpoint.

Unsubscribe → honored instantly, permanently removed from all future outreach.

Anything sensitive → flagged to you directly before any response goes out.

⏱ Typically within the hour
12
Argus

Every Touchpoint Logged

Every contact is tracked: when we reached out, what was sent, what they replied, what the next action is. No more guessing who's been contacted or when. Your entire outreach history - organized and always current.

⏱ Updated in real time
13
You

Show Up to the Meeting

The prospect already knows who you are - they responded to your email. You walk in warm, not cold. The meeting is already on your calendar with context on who they are and what they said.

⏱ Your only recurring commitment
Phase 3 - Ongoing · Every Month
14
Argus

Monthly Report

Every month you get a clear summary - contacts reached, replies received, and meetings booked. No jargon. Just the numbers that matter and a plain-English read on your pipeline. If anything needs to change in the approach, we reach out directly.

⏱ ~5 min to read
15
Argus

Pipeline Refreshes Automatically

As contacts work through the sequence, we source new prospects to keep volume consistent. Your pipeline never goes quiet. You never have to think about it.

⏱ Automatic, every month
