ArgusReach · How It Works

From first call to first meeting —
here's every step.

A clear picture of what happens when, who does what, and what you can expect. Two colors: blue is you, green is Argus and our team.

Phase 0 — Before We Start
1
You + Us

Agreement Signed & Sending Account Created

We send a simple service agreement covering scope, pricing, and term. Once signed, you pay the $500 setup fee and we get started. You'll create a dedicated outreach email address on your domain (e.g., outreach@yourcompany.com) using Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 — this is the address all outreach goes from. Takes about 10 minutes to set up on your end.

We handle all technical configuration — DKIM, SPF, and domain authentication. You just create the email address and share the credentials with us. We verify everything remotely — no IT access required from our side.

⏱ ~10 min total, one time
2
You

Connect Your Calendar

We set up a dedicated booking link for you — no Calendly account needed on your end. You just connect your Google or Outlook calendar to it. When an interested prospect wants to meet, they book directly onto your calendar. You get the confirmation. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Works with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Microsoft 365. Takes about 2 minutes.

⏱ ~2 min, one time
Phase 1 — Setup · Week 1
3
You

Fill Out Your Intake Form — 10 Minutes

We learn your business through a short intake form — who your best clients are, who you want to reach, your target market, and how you naturally communicate. No call required. Everything after this is handled for you.

⏱ ~10 min, one time
4
You

Share Your Existing Contacts — Optional but Valuable

Warm list: People you've already met, spoken to, or reached out to before — even if it went nowhere. We prioritize these with openers that reference the existing connection.

Active relationships: Anyone you already actively work with who should never receive a cold intro. We remove these automatically before anything sends.

Can be a spreadsheet, a CSV export, or even a rough list of names. We clean and organize it.

⏱ ~15 min if contacts are organized — no rush — send it over whenever it's ready
5
Argus

Build Your Prospect List

Argus sources verified contacts in your target market — matched to the ideal client profile we capture in your intake form. Every contact is verified: name, company, role, and email. Your warm list is folded in and prioritized. Active relationships are removed.

⏱ 1–2 business days
6
Argus

Write Personalized Outreach Sequences

A 3-touch email sequence written in your voice — introducing who you are, what you do, and why a conversation makes sense. Each email's opening references something specific to that prospect — their company, role, or context. Not a template blast. Every message feels like it was written for them.

What's a sequence? A series of short, timed follow-up emails sent automatically over 3–4 weeks. Most people don't respond to the first email — not because they're not interested, but because they're busy. A sequence follows up naturally so no opportunity is lost. Each email is standalone, short, and easy to respond to.

⏱ 1–2 business days
7
You + Us

Review and Approve

We share the complete email sequence with you before a single email sends. Read through it, edit anything, change the tone — whatever you need. Nothing goes out without your sign-off. Most clients take 15–20 minutes on this step.

⏱ ~15–20 min
Phase 2 — Live · Week 2 Onward
8
Argus

Campaign Goes Live

Emails send from your outreach address on a human-like schedule — not a blast, not a bot pattern. Follow-ups go out automatically to non-responders at timed intervals. Every email looks like it came directly from you. Monthly billing begins on this date.

⏱ Runs automatically, 24/7
9
Argus

Every Reply Is Handled

Argus monitors and classifies every reply in real time:

Interested → booking link sent immediately. The prospect books directly onto your calendar — you get an instant confirmation with their name, company, and meeting time. No action needed from you.
Not now → logged with a follow-up date, re-queued for a future sequence.
Unsubscribe → honored instantly, contact added to permanent do-not-contact list.
Anything sensitive → flagged to you directly, no auto-response sent.

⏱ Typically within the hour
10
Argus

Every Touchpoint Logged Automatically

Every contact is tracked: when we reached out, what was sent, what they replied, and what the next action is. No more guessing who's been contacted or when. Your entire outreach history — organized, searchable, always current.

⏱ Updated in real time
11
You

Show Up to the Meeting

The prospect already knows who you are — they responded to your email. You walk in warm, not cold. The meeting is on your calendar with context on who they are, what they replied, and any prior outreach history.

⏱ Your only recurring commitment
Phase 3 — Ongoing · Every Month
12
Argus

Monthly Report + Optimization

Every month you receive a clear activity summary: contacts reached, replies received, meetings booked, contacts pending follow-up, and what we're changing next month based on what worked. No jargon — just the numbers that matter and a plain-English summary of your pipeline. The system gets sharper every cycle.

⏱ ~5 min to read
13
Argus

Pipeline Refreshes Automatically

As the contact list works through the sequence, Argus sources new prospects to keep volume consistent. Your pipeline never goes quiet. You never have to think about it.

⏱ Automatic, every month
