ArgusReach · Workflow

From first call to live campaign.Here is exactly how it works.

A clear picture of what happens when, what we handle for you, and what you can expect from strategy call through launch.

What to expect

You are not buying software you have to manage.

ArgusReach is done for you. We handle the operational side, but there are a few key approvals and setup items on your side before outreach goes live.

  • Agreement, setup fee, and intake come first
  • Dedicated outreach email and calendar connection happen during setup
  • Launch happens only after all pre-launch gates are green
Phase 1

Sales and intake

This is the front-end onboarding phase. Nothing launches yet. The goal here is to make sure the agreement is signed, the setup fee is paid, and your intake information is captured correctly.

What happens

Discovery call, agreement, setup fee, intake

After the discovery call, Vito sends the service agreement, the $500 setup fee link, and your intake form. Once those are complete, the intake can be approved in the portal.

  • You sign the agreement
  • You pay the $500 setup fee
  • You submit the intake form
  • Argus gets the information needed to build your campaign
Phase 2

Intake approval and setup

01

Intake gets approved

After payment and signature are verified, the intake is approved. That approval automatically creates your client record, writes your DNC information, generates your unique subscription link, creates your secure credential link, sends your welcome email, and generates your first outreach sequence draft.

02

You set up your outreach email and submit credentials

You create a dedicated outreach email account, generate an app password, and submit both through the secure one-time credential link. This is how Argus gets access to the sending mailbox safely.

03

Warmup and DNS setup begin

Once credentials are received, Argus links them to your client record, starts Instantly warmup immediately, generates DNS records for your sending domain, and starts DNS verification polling in the background.

04

Sequence review and follow-up email

Vito reviews and edits your generated sequence draft if needed, then sends the follow-up email with your DNS records, your sequence draft, your Calendly link, and warmup timing expectations.

05

Client-side setup runs in parallel

While warmup runs, your side handles the remaining setup steps: IT adds DNS records, you review and approve the sequence, and you connect your calendar to Calendly.

Phase 3

Pre-launch gates

Nothing launches early. ArgusReach checks all six launch gates before the campaign is considered ready.

All 6 required

Your campaign is only ready when these are green

  • ICP configured
  • Sequence approved
  • DNS verified
  • Warmup score at or above 85%
  • Subscription payment confirmed
  • Calendar connected

When all six are green, the portal marks the client ready to launch and alerts Vito. Vito then sends the ready-to-launch email with your unique subscription link.

Phase 4

Launch and active campaign

01

Subscription payment is confirmed

After the client pays the monthly subscription, Vito confirms payment and creates the campaign once all six gates are satisfied.

02

Argus builds the campaign draft automatically

The launch pipeline pulls leads from Apollo using your ICP, filters DNC contacts, replaces removed leads when needed, personalizes the outreach with company-level context, and creates the Instantly campaign as a draft with your approved sequence and lead set loaded.

03

Vito reviews and activates in Instantly

Argus does not auto-activate. Vito reviews the draft in Instantly first. Once activated, Gob marks the client active and the monitor goes live.

04

Reply handling and booking run automatically

Instantly sends during business hours. The monitor filters warmup traffic, classifies genuine replies, alerts Vito when attention is needed, drafts replies for approval, logs outcomes, and routes booked meetings through Calendly alerts.

05

Voice follow-up activates for Growth and Scale

For Growth and Scale clients, AI voice follow-up can engage prospects who have not replied after the email sequence, using the approved positioning and context from the email campaign.

What Argus handles

  • Client record creation and setup automation
  • Sequence generation and campaign drafting
  • Warmup, DNS polling, and checklist monitoring
  • Reply classification, drafting, and DNC handling
  • Monthly campaign refresh and reporting

What the client still does

  • Create the dedicated outreach email
  • Provide the app password securely
  • Have IT add DNS records
  • Approve the sequence and connect the calendar
  • Show up to warm conversations and close
Phase 5

Monthly cycle

On the first of each month, Argus refreshes the campaign automatically. New leads are pulled, exclusions are respected, personalization runs again, and new leads are loaded into the active campaign. A monthly report is generated in the portal for Vito to review and send manually.
